#65 [PROCEDURES]: What is a flood-in-progress claim?
  Expected: ["still rising","not receded","ongoing"]
  Status: 200
  Answer: A **flood-in-progress claim** is one where the SFIP will NOT pay because the flood causing the damage was a continuation of a flood that existed **before the policy became effective or before the application was submitted**.

## Key Rule (SFIP V.B)

The exclusion applies regardless of:
- Property, city, county, or parish boundaries
- Whether floodwaters physically touched the building
- How much time passed between the initial flood and when damage occurred
